Route and Station Feedback

Short traveler reports can help confirm which stops are dependable, which stations need caution, and which backup options are worth listing.

Useful feedback

  • Route: Include the corridor, direction, and major leg. Example: Salt Lake City to Los Angeles, I-15 southbound.
  • Station: Include the station name, city, nearby business, and charger network when known.
  • Access: Note gates, blocked entrances, parking limits, after-hours access, and whether staff activation was needed.
  • Charging result: Say whether it worked, failed, charged slowly, required an app, required an adapter, or had a long wait.
  • Recommendation: Mark the stop as primary, backup only, avoid for now, or needs more verification.

Publishing rules

  • Date matters: Station access, pricing, and reliability can change quickly.
  • No private details: Avoid license plates, personal names, phone numbers, or photos of other drivers.
  • Moderate first: Treat user reports as leads until the route segment is reviewed and verified.
