EV Forums

General EV forums

  • InsideEVs Forum: General EV discussion, charging topics, trip reports, and model-specific sections. Open InsideEVs Forum
  • Reddit r/electricvehicles: Broad EV discussion, news, owner questions, and community opinions. Open r/electricvehicles

Vehicle-specific forums

  • Tesla Motors Club: Tesla-focused owner discussion, charging network topics, and model-specific threads. Open Tesla Motors Club
  • IONIQ Forum: Hyundai IONIQ owner discussion, including IONIQ 5, IONIQ 6, charging, service, and ownership questions. Open IONIQ Forum

Use forum advice carefully

  • Check the post date. Charging access, adapters, software, and station reliability can change quickly.
  • Look for repeated reports from several drivers instead of relying on one comment.
  • Verify route and station details with the charging app, station map, vehicle manual, or network support before a long trip.
  • Forums are helpful for real-world clues, but they are not a substitute for current station status.
