Charging Locations

EV charging can happen at home, work, public stops, destinations, and along travel corridors. Each location type serves a different purpose.

Home

Usually the most convenient place for routine charging if parking and electrical access are available.

Workplace

Useful for commuters, shared vehicles, and drivers who park for several hours.

Public

Grocery stores, libraries, parking garages, hotels, campuses, and downtown areas may offer public charging.

Corridor

Fast chargers near highways and main routes help with longer trips between towns and cities.

Destination

Hotels, parks, restaurants, and venues can provide charging while drivers spend time there.

Fleet or Depot

Some organizations charge vehicles at private lots, yards, warehouses, or service locations.
