FAQs

Short answers to common EV questions, written for everyday drivers rather than sales conversations.

Common questions

  • How long does charging take?
    It depends on the vehicle, battery level, charger speed, temperature, and how full you need the battery to be.
  • Do I need a special charger at home?
    Many drivers use Level 1 for light driving or Level 2 for faster daily charging.
  • Is fast charging always best?
    Fast charging is useful on trips, but slower home or workplace charging is often more convenient for daily use.
  • What should I check before a trip?
    Check charger availability, connector type, backup stops, weather, elevation, and the vehicle's estimated range.
